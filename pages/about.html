Our Story

People helping people, one step at a time.

Global Steps was founded on the belief that meaningful change is built locally and scaled globally — through partnership, patience, and a shared commitment to dignity.

Founded 2014

Why we exist

More than 700 million people still live in extreme poverty. We believe the answers already exist within communities — they need partners who listen, fund, and stay for the long haul.

Since 2014 we've worked in 25 countries with community-led organizations to advance education, health, sustainable livelihoods, and climate resilience.

Mission & Vision

🎯

Mission

To empower communities through education, sustainable development, and collaborative action.

🌍

Vision

A world where every community has the resources and support to thrive.

Our Values

🤝

Partnership first

We work alongside communities, never around them. Solutions are co-designed, owned, and led locally.

🔗

Long-term commitment

Real change takes years. We commit to multi-year partnerships rather than one-off interventions.

📊

Evidence & transparency

Every program is measured, every dollar accounted for. Our annual reports are public.

🌿

Climate-aware

Every program considers environmental impact and helps build resilience to a changing climate.

Our Journey

2014

Founded in Lisbon

Three friends launched Global Steps with a single education partnership in rural Kenya.

2017

Expanded to South Asia

Reached 10 countries and launched our community health initiative.

2020

Pandemic response

Delivered emergency relief to 60,000 households across our partner network.

2023

Climate program launched

Began our sustainability and climate resilience portfolio across 8 countries.

2026

10,000+ lives impacted

Now working with 50+ community-led partners in 25 countries.
