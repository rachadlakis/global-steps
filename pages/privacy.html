Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. Who we are

Global Steps NGO ("we", "us") is a registered nonprofit organization. This policy describes how we collect and use personal information through our website and donor services.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when you donate, sign up for our newsletter, apply to volunteer, contact us, or apply for a job. This may include name, email, postal address, phone, and (for donations) payment information processed by our PCI-compliant payment provider.

We also collect basic analytics: pages visited, browser type, country-level location. We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not set tracking cookies.

3. How we use information

  • Process donations and provide receipts
  • Send newsletters and program updates (only if you opt in)
  • Respond to your questions, applications, and inquiries
  • Improve our website and measure program reach
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with service providers (payment processing, email delivery, CRM) under data-processing agreements, and where required by law.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. EU/UK residents have additional rights under GDPR. To exercise your rights, email privacy@globalsteps.ngo.

6. Retention

We keep donor records for as long as legally required (typically 7 years) and other personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards. No system is perfectly secure; we take reasonable steps and notify affected individuals of any breach as required by law.

8. Children

Our website is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We'll update this page when our practices change. Material changes will be highlighted on the homepage for 30 days.

10. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@globalsteps.ngo or write to us at Rua das Janelas Verdes, 1200-690 Lisboa, Portugal.
