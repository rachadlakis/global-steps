What We Do

Four programs. One goal: lasting community change.

Our work is built around four interconnected pillars. Each program is co-designed with local partners and measured against transparent outcomes.

Program 01

Education

We partner with schools and community learning centers to expand access to quality education for children and adults who have historically been left out.

  • Teacher training and curriculum design
  • Scholarships for 2,400+ students annually
  • Digital literacy labs in 38 schools
  • Adult literacy programs in 9 countries

Program 02

Sustainability & Climate

Climate change is hitting the communities we work with first and hardest. We fund local adaptation and regenerative practices.

  • Reforestation across 1,200 hectares
  • Solar microgrids serving 14 villages
  • Climate-resilient farming training
  • Water harvesting and rainwater systems
Support Sustainability
Program 03

Community Development

We invest in infrastructure and local economies — bridges, marketplaces, microenterprise grants, and cooperative training.

  • Microenterprise grants for 1,800+ entrepreneurs
  • Cooperative formation and training
  • Women-led business accelerator
  • Community infrastructure (water, roads, markets)

Program 04

Health & Wellness

Access to basic healthcare changes everything. We support clinics, mobile outreach, and maternal health programs.

  • Mobile clinics reaching 65,000 people/year
  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Mental health peer-support networks
  • Clean water and sanitation initiatives
Support Health

How we measure success

Every program has clear outcome targets, baseline measurements, and annual third-party evaluation. We publish results — including what didn't work.
