What didn't work

In 2025, our pilot textbook-distribution program in two regions failed to improve learning outcomes. Independent evaluation showed that without teacher training and follow-up, books alone weren't enough. We've ended that pilot and reallocated funding to teacher development. We share these results because honest accounting is part of doing good work.
